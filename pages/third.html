Xbox Series x


Description:

Xbox Series X is the latest Microsoft console, delivering high-end gaming with reduced loading times, 4K graphics, enhanced performance, and all with Xbox Game Pass at its core.


The exclusives of the Xbox Series X games are amazing with beautiful graphics, exciting stories and more. Games created with the Xbox Series X|S development kit have much faster load times and amazing visuals at up to 120 frames per second.


Microsoft's new consoles allow you a lot more flexibility in how you play its new games and The Xbox Series X is significantly more powerful than the Xbox Series S or the Xbox One, which has a significant impact on performance.


New generation of consoles brings significant improvements to gaming, and Microsoft's successors to the Xbox One are no exception. Games appear better, thanks to more powerful graphics hardware and built-in support for more realistic lighting technology, and feel more responsive, thanks to support for frame rates up to 120 frames per second
